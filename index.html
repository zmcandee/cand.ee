Things I Actually Use

  • Yoto

    Screen-free audio player for kids — audiobooks, podcasts, and music on physical cards with no ads or algorithms. A genuinely great device for young families.

    yotoplay.com referral
  • US Mobile

    Prepaid cellphone MVNO that supports all the major US carriers. Great pricing for limited data plans and not uncompetive in the unlimited category. You have to buy your own phone and accept that there is no in person support.

    usmobile.com referral
  • Fastmail

    Simple email you pay for with good SPAM protection, excellent filtering, and alias support.

    fastmail.com referral
  • Eero

    The just works wireless router option perfect for your grandma but if you ever configure more than your WiFi password you probably would be happier with Ubiquiti.

    eero.com
  • YNAB

    Zero-based or envelope budgeting. Expensive but simpler collaboration than a spreadsheet and includes importing from most banks.

    ynab.com referral
